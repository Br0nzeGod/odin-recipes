Hot Honey Chicken Tenders

Ingrediaents:

  • 8-10 chicken tenders
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup franks red hot (cayenne)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Flour Mixture:

  • 3/4 cup AP flour
  • 1/2 cup corn starch
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Hot Honey Sauce:

  • 1 stick unsalted butter (8 tbsp/113g)
  • 1/2 cup franks red hot (cayenne)
  • 1 tbsp packed brown sugar
  • 1/8 cup honey

Steps:

  1. Mix together your marinade and allow your chicken to chill in it for at least one hour.
  2. When youre ready to start frying prepare your buttermilk mixture and flour miixture.
  3. Next, take your marinade chicekn and coat it in your flour mixture, then intot your buttermilk mixture, then one last time into your flour mixture.
  4. Allow your coated chicken tenders to rest for 10 minutes before you fry them.
  5. Fry them on low-medium heat (about 350) for about 6 minutes or until they are fully cooked.
  6. While your chicken is frying, add the ingredients for your hot honey suace into a small sauce pan and melt together on low heat.
  7. Once your chicken is done frying, brush your hot honey sauce on your tenders.
